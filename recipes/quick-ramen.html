Quick Ramen

Easy homemade chicken ramen, with a flavorful broth, roasted chicken, fresh veggies,
lots of noodles, and a soft cooked egg. Inspired by traditional Japanese ramen, but on the table in under an hour.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • Salted Butter
  • Sesame Oil
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook Noodles
  2. Boil Chicken
  3. Add chicken with ramen
